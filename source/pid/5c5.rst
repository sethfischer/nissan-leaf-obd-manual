Equations
~~~~~~~~~

Odometer reading :eq:`5c5-odometer`:

.. math::
    :label: 5c5-odometer

    Given:

    byteB &= 0_{16} = 0_{2}

    byteC &= 9B_{16} = 10011011_{2}

    byteD &= DC_{16} = 11011100_{2}

    Odometer\ reading &= 0_{2}(2^{16}) + 10011011_{2}(2^8) + 11011100_{2}

    &= 10011011\ 11011100_{2} = 9BDC_{16}

    &= 39900\ km
