Calculation
~~~~~~~~~~~

.. math::

    Given:

    B &= 0_{16} = 0_{2}

    C &= 9B_{16} = 10011011_{2}

    D &= DC_{16} = 11011100_{2}

    Odometer\ reading &= 0_{2}(2^{16}) + 10011011_{2}(2^8) + 11011100_{2}

    &= 10011011\ 11011100_{2} = 9BDC_{16}

    &= 39900 km
